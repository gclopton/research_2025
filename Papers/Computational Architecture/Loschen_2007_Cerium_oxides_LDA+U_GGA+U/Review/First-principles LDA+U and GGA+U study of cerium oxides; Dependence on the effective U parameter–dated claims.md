# Dated claims — First-principles LDA+U and GGA+U study of cerium oxides; Dependence on the effective U parameter

## Systematic study of U_eff

Original statement (verbatim from the paper note before update):

> Although some work in this direction has been published rather recently, i.e., the determination of $\mathrm{U}_{\text {eff }}$ in an empirical way for $\mathrm{CeO}_{2}$ with the PBE functional, ${ }^{6}$ a more systematic study is needed

Status (as of Feb 2026): outdated (systematic benchmarks and reviews now exist; the remaining issue is transferability of a single $\mathrm{U}_{\text {eff }}$ across properties/environments/implementations).

Updated inline wording used in the paper note:

> Although some work in this direction has been published rather recently, i.e., the determination of $\mathrm{U}_{\text {eff }}$ in an empirical way for $\mathrm{CeO}_{2}$ with the PBE functional, ${ }^{6}$ subsequent benchmark studies and reviews have expanded substantially; they also make clear that first-principles strategies for estimating $\mathrm{U}$ (e.g., linear-response and DFPT-based approaches) are available, but the resulting effective $\mathrm{U}$ can remain functional-, projector-, and property-dependent, so empirical calibration or property-targeted validation remains common in ceria modeling. ${ }^{58,61,62}$
