# Paper Index: Actinide Context, Surrogacy & Stockpile Stewardship

This index collects papers that motivate why ceria matters as more than just a convenient oxide for track simulations. It covers ceria as a surrogate for plutonium oxides, radiation-tolerant ceramics and waste-form thinking around plutonium immobilization, and stockpile-stewardship literature on plutonium aging. These papers are adjacent to the main ceria-track project rather than central to the SHI mechanism itself, so they live in a separate companion index.

**When to reach for this index:** You are writing broader motivation, justifying why CeO2 is used as a surrogate for actinide systems, situating the project within nuclear-materials relevance, or tracing the stockpile-stewardship / waste-form context that makes ceria and related ceramics strategically important.
