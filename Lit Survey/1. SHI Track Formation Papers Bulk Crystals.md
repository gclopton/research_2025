## Diamond (bulk crystal)


- **Amekura et al. (2024), “Latent ion tracks were finally observed in diamond”** — **[Experiment + simulation]** First clear observation of _latent ion tracks_ in diamond using 2–9 MeV C(_{60}) cluster ions (tracks visible by TEM; amorphization + sp(^2) signal).
