## ** Major Performance Issues Identified:**

1. System Size Problem
- Ce7012: 57 atoms (21 Ce + 360 O)
- CeO2: 192 atoms (64 Ce + 128 O)
- Ce203: 135 atoms (45 Ce + 90 0)

Ce7012 is actually the SMALLEST system, so size isn't the issue.
2. Critical INCAR Parameter Issues

LREAL = .FALSE. (Line 25)
- Problem: This forces reciprocal-space projection operators
- VASP Warning: "You have a (more or less) 'large supercell' and for larger cells it might be more efficient to use real-space projection operators. Therefore, try LREAL = Auto"
- Impact: This is likely the main cause of slowness

NPAR = 4 (Line 28)
- Problem: Too low for 64 cores
- Optimal: Should be closer to sqrt(64) = 8, or even higher for better parallelization

ENCUT = 400 (Line 3)
- Problem: May be too low for Ce7012 stoichiometry
- CeO2 uses: Higher cutoff (likely 500+)

3. Job Failures
- Recent job: Failed at RMM step 56 with energy explosion
- Pattern: Jobs are crashing during electronic convergence
- Cause: Poor convergence due to suboptimal parameters
